How to send Message to other Readers?

  • STEP 1
    You need to note down your ID first. You can find them in your "Profile".
  • STEP 2
    You need to note down Receiver's ID now. Other user's ID usually mentioned in the list.
  • STEP 3
    Go to "Compose".
  • STEP 4
    Write / Compose your message here ! Fill all the details carefully!
  • STEP 5
    SEND IT!!

How to see your Messeges?

  • STEP 1
    Go to your "Message" from your Dashboard.
  • STEP 2
    Go to your "Inbox"
  • STEP 3
    There you go!! All Your Messages are here!!
